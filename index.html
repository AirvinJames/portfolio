Education

2015 - 2020

In 2015, I started school at Casa del Bambino Emmanuel Montessori. There I began my educational journey. During this time, I actively took part in academic competitions, such as the MTAP (Mathematics Teachers Association of the Philippines) math challenge. It was an exciting and valuable part of my education that tested my problem-solving skills and fueled my passion for mathematics. My years at Casa del Bambino were marked by exploration, hands-on learning, and an environment that encouraged curiosity and independence. These experiences laid the foundation for my future endeavors, shaping my love for learning and setting the stage for my academic path ahead.

2020 - 2022

From 2020 to 2022, I attended Kumintang Elementary School, spanning my time from Grade 5 to graduation in Grade 6. The shift to this school was caused by the challenges posed by COVID-19, making it a closer and more convenient option. During my time there, I actively participated in various competitions, including the Division School's Press Conference. The culmination of this period was my graduation as a salutatorian. Moreover, these years marked the beginning of my journey as a programmer. I embarked on learning Python and using Pygame to create small games, initiating my exploration into the world of programming.

2022 - Today

In 2022, I embarked on a new academic chapter as a Grade 7 high school student at Batangas State University - The National Engineering University. This transition marked a significant step forward in my educational journey, and I eagerly embraced the opportunities it presented. Since joining, I've actively participated in competitions such as the 41st Karibok ang Tuktok and 25th PMO, which have not only challenged my skills but also enriched my learning experience.
Presently, I'm a Grade 8 student in this School and I'm excited to continue my education here. The school's focus on STEM aligns perfectly with my interests, and I look forward to further growth and learning in this school.
